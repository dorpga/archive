WARNING: Don't get PUPs!

by WindowsAstro6369 » Sat Sep 27, 2014 5:03 am

If you mainly are on Windows, and you frequently download software (0-2 per month), PUPs (or Potentially Unwanted Programs) are out there, and can catch you if you don't decline. They come in freeware/shareware on sites such as Download.com or Softonic. Sometimes they do not come from there. They can be toolbars and browser hijackers.

Once, near the start of 2014, I got a PUP show with Optimizer Pro, 24x7 Support (or something), Conduit and more. Just so you know, it's happened to me and recently I had removed the traces that were leftover with MBAM.
