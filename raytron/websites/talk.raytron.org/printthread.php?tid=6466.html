Ok, lets be realistic here... when you plug a webcam into your system for the first time on Windows 10 it may send a large chunk of data to the Microsoft servers but this doesn't mean that it is a picture, in fact, why would they even want/need a picture in the first place?

A company like this can't do much with a picture of you or anything else when you plug in your webcam.
