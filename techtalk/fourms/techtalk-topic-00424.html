Re: Emulators on a Computer or Physical Console?

by maxell » Wed Jun 18, 2014 7:07 am

...emulators don't just make your computer think it's a console, or really have much to do with consoles at all. They actually simulate a different kind of processor, essentially like a programming language interpreter, except it interprets another machine's raw binary code into x86 code (or ARM, or 68k, or whatever you're using).

Consoles are computers, PCs are computers, smartphones are computers. Some people even think you're a computer. So sit down at your x86 AMD rig with FreeBSD installed on it, and play some PS4 games!
