In an attempt to revive the old forum community, major changes are going to take place in the next few months.

"techTalk 3.0" will not use phpbb or mybb. It will use forum software written by the community from scratch in a way that it's heavily geared towards the type of community techTalk has been.

In case anybody is wondering I have a small FAQ:

  • Why are you doing this? An attempt to revive the old forum community.
  • Is it open source? Most likely it will be open source, as it is community made. If you are interested in helping feel free to join in.
  • What language will it be written in? I believe JS, but it could be Ruby.
  • What will it be like? It will kind of be like a giant subreddit, without the rest of reddit.
  • Will we still have our old login info? phillips1012 is going to see if he can export our login info from phpbb to the new site. If that is possible, when you first login to techTalk 3.0, you will be given the option to change your username and password (As long as it is community appropriate)
  • Why not use mybb? Because a bunch of separate boards on a community as small as ours doesn't really feel like a community.
