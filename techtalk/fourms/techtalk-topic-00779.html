Who has used PS2 Linux?

by RainfallMuffin » Sun Aug 24, 2014 11:32 pm

Linux for Playstation 2 (AKA PS2 Linux) is a kit released by Sony in 2002 that let you run Linux on the PS2. It included a Linux-based operating system, a USB keyboard and mouse, a VGA adapter, a PS2 network adaptor (Ethernet only), and a 40 GB hard disk drive (HDD). There is also another distro known as BlackRhino. Both the kit and BlackRhino are discontinued.

So, who has used either of these?
