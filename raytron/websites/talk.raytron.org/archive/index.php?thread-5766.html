Hey all. I have made the official techTalk minecraft server along with phillips1012. The server runs on my own personal laptop, and it has a lot of mods. It's on minecraft 1.6.4 and uses the FTB: Tech World modpack with OpenComputers and PneumaticCraft added to make it as techy as possible. Regular backups are performed. The thunder season is coming to Oregon, so expect some downtime if a storm occurs.
You need the technic launcher to use this server:

It should take about a minute on the first run since it has to create all the config files for the mods.

The rank system is similar to the ranks here on the forum:

phillips1012 & KomputerKid are the Owners of the server. We have all rights.
All Senior Moderators and non-founder Admins are the Admins of the server. They kick in if phillips or myself are not around. They also have all rights.
All Moderators and Chat Moderators are the moderators of the server. They are responsible for keeping the server clean.
All former VIP members are VIPs on the server. They have some extra permissions like /heal and /fly. (The VIP usergroup has been removed from the forum, however VIPs will stay on the server)
Everybody else is Members, and Wembers are well, Wembers on the server. (Wembers on the server cannot build, all they can do is kill themselves)
phillips1012 is the master of RF energy. Producing your own energy is pointless when you can get energy from phillips1012's nuclear reactor and steam turbine for the cheap price of 8 enderium ingots and 1 diamond
