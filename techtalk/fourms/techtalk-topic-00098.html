Re: Should the internet be free of charge?

by allstarsnorks2 » Mon May 26, 2014 8:53 am

I don't think the idea of a costless internet is a good idea. Some ISPs in the world are trying to keep quality with quantity. What that means is that the ISPs need to upgrade their technology just to allow faster internet, which will attract some consumers who are interested in signing up for their services. But upgrading costs money, which consumers have to pay through monthly subscription costs. If nobody pays for service, the ISP would not have enough money to upgrade their technology. This will therefore make the ISP behind time, and make potential subscribers flee to other ISPs.
