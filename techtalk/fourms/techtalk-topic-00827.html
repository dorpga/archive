Why you should never paste arbitrary text into your terminal

by phillips1012 » Sun Sep 07, 2014 9:46 pm

Pasting text into your terminal is basically typing out what you pasted into the program running within. All of the keystrokes and key combinations can be encoded and represented in html.

Here's a demo I made of what a malicious attacker could do: (only works on unix-likes, no damage or privacy invasion is actually done)
http://pasteht.ml/h756L

The text that executes the commands is hidden with CSS, but is still copied. This also works with commands, of course: http://thejh.net/misc/website-terminal-copy-paste
