How to save your post when Cloudflare checks browser

by djdeedahx » Sat Jun 21, 2014 8:47 pm

I've heard that people have started complaining of CloudFlare deleting posts that are being worked on. That won't happen anymore with this trick!

When CloudFlare starts checking your browser after you've clicked Submit, IMMEDIATELY PRESS THE BACK BUTTON.

Do NOT click Submit again. Now Ctrl+C your post, then press Submit. Let it check your browser again.

You will go to a blank post page. Ctrl+V your post, then press Submit.

Hope this works!

(Again, please do not ask to disable CloudFlare. I might be sensing some suspicious people wanting to DDoS us again. You never know.
