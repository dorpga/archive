Samsung T24C300

by jakeypearce » Sat May 24, 2014 12:08 pm

I have a Samsung T24C300 and it is a nice monitor. I bought it to replace my old Samsung SyncMaster 2032BW because it had a small resolution (1680x1050), no HDMI support (I wanted to have to my PS3 set up in the same room as my tower), and I wanted integrated speakers to replace my old Logitech speakers!

It is a nice monitor with support for 1080P and supports the PS3 which is really nice for me! Going to the source is quite easy as you have two ways. You can do it with the remote or on the monitor itself! Integrated speakers also means I won't have to worry about cables!

The only downsides are that it has an annoying eco mode saver option and the remote is hard to use too!

Overall, a nice monitor to use!
