When I was 8 years old we had battles of NES vs Wii.

(We didn't know they were made by the same company back then)

It was like:

Me: The Wii has a remote you can use to play sports like real life!
Friend: But the NES is cheaper and it doesn't need internet!
Me: But the Wii can view websites!
Friend: And the NES controller doesn't need batteries! And discs (we called them NES discs even though they were cartridges) don't get scratched!

I then bought a NES at age 9 (a real Nintendo one too) and played it so much that I think my Wii became jealous. I also had a 31-in-1 cartridge, so I didn't have to worry about shop channel crap. Another NES anecdote:

Me: Hey! Wanna play some video games? I have a NES and a DS!
n00b: I am way better than you! I have a 999999-in-1 cartridge!
Me: Those are all rip-offs I've played one. It only has like 5 games and they repeat over and over!
n00b: You're making that up because you're jealous.
Me: Okay, compare that 999999-in-1 cartridge to my 31-in-1 and if we see any games repeat we'll play DS download play Pokemon diamond and pearl.

He didn't have diamond and pearl. He became friends with the douchebag that had a PS3.
