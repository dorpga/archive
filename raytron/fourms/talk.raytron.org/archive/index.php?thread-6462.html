Yesterday YouTube released YouTube Gaming, a gaming live streaming service that is supposed to compete with Twitch.

I've been playing around with it, checking people out, watching streams, and it's pretty cool. It's definitely faster than Twitch and doesn't buffer as much (I didn't have buffering issues with Twitch, but I did have quality issues) It also has a large library of games that you can follow, more than I was expecting, actually. You can also watch videos by Gaming Youtubers that you can also watch on YouTube. The only thing I don't like is the layout. I like the Twitch app layout better. I find the YT Gaming layout a bit confusing, but I've been getting the hang of it.

Final thoughts: will it be successful? I believe so, since quality of the stream is better and it was released by YouTube. However, there are still a bunch of Youtubers streaming on Twitch, so I think it will be a while until they switch over, if they ever do. I don't believe YouTube offers subscriptions like Twitch does, which could keep people from switching for now. But overall, not bad for a first try YouTube.
