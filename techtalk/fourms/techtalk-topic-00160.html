I am psychobat, most people call me psycho or physco due to my old username, physcobat (corrected the spelling for the current username). I was a mod of the dovernus forums before it was taken down and I was a former mod and former admod of the former osft forums. I usually spend my time either on the computer or fixing various things around the house. My mom recently got a new car to replace the shitty chevy cavalier we used to have, so me and my mom have been taking more road trips to the beach or to a campground or places like that since we now have enough room so that we dont have to sacrifice comfort to fit everything in the car. Well, I think thats enough about me for the moment.

Now, it is time to get onto my point of view of what happened. Right around the time osft gave up on the former osft forums and gave it to vortex, I decided I wasnt going to hold in my opinions any longer. I wont lie, from the beginning, I was siding with bribob, but I held in those opinions because I wanted to keep my position on the forums team. I got tired of the former osft forum getting spammed non stop, so I decided to act on my beliefs. I perdicted that I would get demoted and/or banned for acting on them, but I predicted it would push the community in the right direction. My actions were never meant for harm, even though there were times I will admit I was a bit harsh. It started by bribob offering to make a replacement forum for the former osft forum. I didnt see a problem with it and me, bribob, and uber somewhat teamed up and made the former dovernus forums. It took us about 4-7 days to finish and it was a hit at first. A day or two before it was finished, I unbanned cirno and bribob from the former osft forums because there was no proof to back there banned and I was pissed that they got banned. First off, dovernus never tried to "steal" members from the osft forums, the goal of dovernus was to give members a second option for the ones either fed up with how the forum was ran or the ones who were sick of freeforums, it was never meant for harm. Also, I was not demoted and I sure as hell wasnt banned for promoting the dovernus forums in the announcements, I was demoted about a week or two after I promoted it and I was stuck in the unwelcome members section a 3-7 days after my demotion. My advertizement deemed dovernus as the unofficial replacement because I knew not everyone liked bribob and it wasnt an osft forum. Of course, I probably should of put it in the off topic section, but I thought as long as I put it as the unofficial replacement that not too many would mind if I stuck it in the announcements and tons of members advertized their forum on the osft forum with no punishments. Getting back on track so I dont make this miserably long and boring...........

The dovernus forums opened up and I advertized it as I stated above. As a result, I got my account deactivated since for some reason, osft thought my account was hacked. I cleared it up with him and proved I wasnt hacked and i got it reactivated. At that point, he recently given up on the forum and gave it to vortex.At first, everything seemed okay, but it turned into a case that you were either on dovernus or you were on the former ostalk with no middle ground. Now, I predicted what was going to happen. The rise of dovernus was either going to replace the forum and the members from the former ostalk who didnt trust it would go somewhere else and ostalk would mostly crumble or everyone from ostalk was going to find a way to get off of freeforums without dovernus. Either way, I knew that osfirsttimer.freeforums.org would die and be replaced, which was mostly my goal to see that forum die so the community there wouldnt have to suffer with freeforums any longer. Once ostalk died and tech talk was born, dovernus died shortly after that. That is most of the way I see things, I apologize if I said some harsh things before and did some minimal spamming, it had to be done. If I left anything out or anyone has questions, feel free to email me at [email protected] or if it is possible, I would like it if my ban would be delayed for a couple days, if it can, then just reply to this and I will answer questions on this thread, but if I am going to get banned right away like I think I am, just email me your questions and I will answer it as soon as I can.

Now, I wont lie, I do not regret at all what I did and I wouldnt do things differently if I could go back in time. I believe that if I didnt rebel, we would still be on freeforums and freeforums is hell in my opinion and possibly many others opinions and I believed that this community needed a push to get away from freeforums. A lot of my actions were to help this forum and not hurt this forum, even though there were points in time I got too carried away with it.

Now, the reason why I didnt sign up with the name psychobat was because I knew I would be banned before I would finish typing this and I do not want to have to break the ban evasion rule to post this. I will not show my face on this forum ever again if that's what everyone wants, which I assume that's what everyone wants. I would however like everyone to consider giving me a second chance by letting me be a regular member. If I can get a second chance, I will be keeping this account and I would not make a second account to get my old username back, even though I would like it if I could get a username change. I will again deeply apologize to anyone I said harsh things to or offended, but as I said tons before in this message, my intentions were to push this forum in the right direction so it wouldnt die at freeforums. I am about ready to end this thread as for it has gone on far too long, I hope everybody reads this fully, forgives the harsh things I said, and considers giving me a second chance.
