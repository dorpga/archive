On linux, most of the free RAM is used for hard drive caching. The gnome system monitor is showing the actual RAM not used for hard drive caching, while conky is reporting the amount of ram used for the programs and hard drive caching. Hard drive caching has no effect on how much RAM is available. http://www.linuxatemyram.com/
