Do you know Speedy Eggbert?

Speedy Eggbert is a game created by EPSITEC and published by eGames. The game presents the yellow-oval-shape character named Eggbert (also known as Blupi), who always finds and collects treasure in these levels. I love to play this game because it is so fun. And I like to build custom levels in the game whenever I am in the mood.
