This is the alternate method that works on any 360 with an available USB port. Good for pranking your friends!
What you will need:
A computer with at least one free USB port
an Xbox 360 (of course)
and a USB thumbstick.

Steps:
Reformat the USB thumbstick.
Stick it in the 360
Have the 360 reformat it again
Stick the newly formatted thumbstick in your PC
Open notepad (or equivalent)
Create a blank text file, save as default.xex
Copy the text file to the USB and overwrite
take out the thumbstick
Put it in the 360
Turn on the 360

If everything went correctly, the 360 will attempt to read the blank default.xex and crash with an e71 error. To fix, take out the USB thumbstick and reboot the 360.
