The iOS 7.1.1 untethered jailbreak has been released!

by T_Dogg_94 » Tue Jun 24, 2014 6:51 pm

Hi there, guys. The iOS 7.1.1 untethered jailbreak has been released, here's what you need to do.
1. Make sure you have iTunes installed.
2. Edit your iPhone's date to June 2, 2014
3. Open the PanGu.exe file
4. IMPORTANT: UNCHECK THE CHECKMARK to the left where you see random characters and the "PP"
5. Click the black button to the right
6. As soon as the "brush stroke" loading bar fills to 20%, the PanGu app will appear on your phone
7. Tap it
8. Select Continue
9. It will fill the loading brush stroke until 80% and your iTunes will open (it will only open IF you have iTunesHelper.exe on your Windows Taskbar)
10. Close iTunes
11. Your device will reboot
12. When it opens again, wait for the brush stroke to complete to 100%
13. Your device will reboot once more
14. The process will be finished 100%
15. The PanGu app will be replaced with Cydia
16. Do your usual stuff by opening Cydia and continue with what you want to install by then.
17. If you have alarms, set them again after the Jailbreak process is complete
