Re: 1080p vs 1440p

by Insyd3 » Mon May 26, 2014 12:59 am

If you are using the screen for productivity I suggest getting a second display rather than a 1440p monitor. One thing to note is that if you are looking for raw quality for tasks live video playback and gaming a 1440p monitor would be a better solution. A 1440p monitor will also be less of a strain on your graphics card than two 1080p monitors.
