turn your non-smart tv into a smart one

by ch_ris » Fri Jul 11, 2014 2:24 pm

Hey guys for this project you will need a lcd tv and a hdmi cable or vga

first connect the vga cable or hdmi to your laptop,
turn on your tv
and go to this website on your laptop http://thedigitallifestyle.com/w/index. ... -tv-setup/
and you need to download relaunch, get it here http://code.google.com/p/relaunch/downloads/list
open relaunch, select custom
and open the with menu select the browser you are using
and for url type in youtube.com/tv,
and click add
and you are done.
alternately you could just open your browser and move it to your tv or second monitor and put it into full screen
and you are done.
