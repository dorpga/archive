techTalk now has site-wide SSL support - phillips1012 - 10-17-2014

Thanks to cloudflare, the caching and security reverse proxy techtalk uses, techTalk now has full SSL support. The connection between my server and cloudflare is encrypted, and the connection between you and cloudflare is encrypted when you use the https URL.

I encourage you to use SSL so your account and your data is secure and immutable by an attacker.
