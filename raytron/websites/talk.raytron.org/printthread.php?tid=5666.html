BASIS 6 Development shelved/BASIS Lite Announcement - Mamidre - 07-22-2014

I am sorry to say, but the development of BASIS 6 will be shelved until further notice.

I'll not make you leave this post with a low note. At the same time, I am happy to say that the development of the 32-bit version of BASIS has started development. Expect the first build to be out within two weeks to month. The base for Lite is either Lubuntu, or some other Ubuntu based lightweight distribution (suggest one down below).
