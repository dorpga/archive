AMD FX-Series... WHY!?

by djdeedahx » Sun Aug 31, 2014 3:32 pm

(Spoiler alert: NOT A HATE TOPIC!)

So I've gotten rid of my AMD hate a few days ago thanks to esc1010 (NOPE, I AM AT IT AGAIN), and I wanted to take a closer at the FX processors from AMD.

So... I came up with these three.

AMD FX 8350
AMD FX 9370
AMD FX 9590

So these three AMD monsters can overclock past 4.8 GHz, have 8 MB of L2 cache (AMD appears to be focusing on L2 cache while Intel focuses on L3 cache), and are ALL unlocked, obviously. But there is one thing that bugs me, that REALLY bugs me...

They are NOT hyperthreaded.

Why, AMD!? You can do much better than this!
