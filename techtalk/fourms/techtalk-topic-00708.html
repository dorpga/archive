EA Access (only on Xbox One)

by allstarsnorks2 » Tue Aug 05, 2014 4:57 pm

Last week, Electronic Arts launched an all-you-can-eat subscription service that's exclusive to the Xbox One. Subscribers to the service will receive unlimited EA games to download and releases of the game days before the actual release. Subscribers will also get 10% off when purchasing DLCs and in-game currency.

This service is in beta and is places are limited. Eventually, this will be available to all Xbox One owners.

Subscription cost:
Monthly (USD $4.99)
Yearly (USD $29.99)

What do you think? Do you think it's worth subscribing to this service? Discuss.
