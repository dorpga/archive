Some thing I dreamt of (just woke up)

by WindowsAstro6369 » Tue Jul 15, 2014 4:56 pm

The farthest thing I can remember:

Two craft falling: left a small one-person plane spacecraft and right a small personal plane about 10x the size of the other one
left parachutes and right lands on runway (there was a hole in the sky pointing to it)

I'm in a research center underwater. I'm wearing a suit, finding all this awesome stuff all over the place. A wind tunnel with a control center. Then I enter somewhere else (no suit required) after thinking, and they talk about disaster. I'm supposed to go into a chamber. There is one that reminds me of a regular room. I am visually told to get into it. I see some guy spitting what appears to be water out of his mouth. Then I can look at the sky, and see a black halo cloud appearing. There is a smaller one above, and again, and again, and again. Through the smallest halo cloud, I could see an asteroid that could barely "fit". It had two craters, reminding me of the two craft falling. I accept the risk.
