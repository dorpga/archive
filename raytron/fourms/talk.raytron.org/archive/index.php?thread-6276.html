techTalk as a forum has been suffering inactivity due to a lack of users wanting to use the forum over the IRC channel.

In an attempt to revive the old forum community, major changes are going to take place in the next few months.

"techTalk 3.0" will not use phpbb or mybb. It will use forum software written by the community from scratch in a way that it's heavily geared towards the type of community techTalk has been.
