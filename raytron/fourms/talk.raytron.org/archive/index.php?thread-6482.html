Haven't been on in a while but I figured it can't hurt to post something. I also know I'm a bit late, I was on a three-day trip to the Ches. Bay when this happened and just got around to this.

Microsoft had an event on Oct. 6 where they unveiled new Lumia phones, new Surfaces, and gave news on the Hololens. Here's a quick recap of what happened if you're interested.

Windows 10
  • Now operates on 110 million PCs
  • MS wants W10 on 1 billion devices in the next few years, which is 11 percent of their goal
  • Facebook making an universal app for W10

Surface Book
  • MS considers it a laptop
  • More in-lap functionality
  • Comes apart so you can use it as a tablet
  • 13.5 in screen, trackpad made of glass, backlit keyboard, and machined magnesium body.
  • Latest Intel Core processor and Nvidia GPU with GDDR5 memory
  • "Fastest laptop of its size ever made"
  • 12 hour battery life and 267 ppi pixel density
  • Keyboard is apparently very quiet
  • Starts at $1,499 US and available for pre-order on Oct. 7
Spoiler
Surface Pro 4
  • Strong iterative improvement on its predecessor.
  • Surface now generates around $3.5 billion in yearly revenue
  • Kept a similar design to the Pro 3 but fitted a larger screen into the space (less bezels)
  • Thinnest Surface Pro to date
  • 12.3 in screen with 267 PPI and supports over 5 mil pixels
  • Stylus now contains a year's worth of battery life and snaps to the edge of the device (no more pen loops)
  • Type Cover is now chiclet-style
  • Retails for $899 US and will be available on Oct. 26
Spoiler
Lumia 950 and Lumia 950 XL
  • Exclusive to AT&T in the US
  • Both come with octacore and hexacore processors and liquid cooling technology.
  • 950 has a 5.2 in screen, XL has 5.7 in display.
  • OLED displays has a pixel density of 564 PPI and 518 PPI respectively.
  • Both has 20 megapixel cameras with natural flash and can shoot 4K video.
  • Comes with 32GB of internal storage, a microSD card slot and a USB Type-C connector
  • Both ship in Nov. 950 will be $549 US and XL will be $649 US.
Spoiler
HoloLens
  • New games for Hololens, such as a shooting game that projects robots on your living room walls.
  • Hololens Dev kit is $3000 US and will be available in Q1 of 2016.
Spoiler
Band 2
  • Sleeker and smarter iteration of its predecessor.
  • More sensitive to touch and features thicker gorilla glass.
  • Able to sense change in elevation and has an improved set of health tracking tools.
  • Available for pre-order for $249 US and will retail on Oct. 30
Spoiler
Xbox One and Windows 10
  • MS will bring W10 to Xbox One later this year
  • Means you will be able to play 360 games on your Xbox One
  • Also announced limited edition consoles inspired by hot games like Halo 5.

Source: http://techcrunch.com/2015/10/06/everyth...are-event/
